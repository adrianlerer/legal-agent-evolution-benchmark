# Draft Message To Anna

Hi Anna,

Your reliability/usefulness distinction, and especially the app-wrapper vs API result, helped me clarify something I have been seeing in my own legal AI work.

I am drafting a short paper/article arguing that legal AI should be benchmarked as induced workflows, not isolated models: model + wrapper + prompt + role + examples + retrieval/context + settings + verifier + human handoff.

The extra dimension I would like to develop is robustness: whether the legal outcome remains stable under reasonable perturbations such as prompt variants, role framing, app/API wrapper, temperature, retrieval on/off, and repeated runs. My intuition is that, in legal work, robustness is part of reliability, not just usefulness.

I am also framing cost as cost per legally acceptable output rather than cost per call.

If useful, I would be happy to share a draft with you before publishing it independently or in a local publication. Your benchmark was one of the triggers, and I would value any critical feedback.
